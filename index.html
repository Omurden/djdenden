Who is DJ Denden?

DJ Denden is a Turkish DJ who has been performing in Istanbul for over 16 years, known for his energetic, nostalgic sets built around Turkish oldies. His signature 90's Turkish Pop Party became one of Istanbul's most popular nightlife events.
